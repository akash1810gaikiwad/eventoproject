SulaFest is India’s biggest vineyard music festival, to be held on 1st & 2nd February, 2020. We fill up the weekend with new and interesting music from across the globe, delectable wines, gourmet food and merchandise from some of the loveliest brands. If all this sounds good already, wait until you hear where SulaFest takes place. Set in Sula’s vineyards in Nashik – the wine capital of India – our festival is propped against the backdrop of rolling hills and surrounded by the calm of the Gangapur lake. At SulaFest 2020, we celebrate our 13th year as a festival and this year we’re going all out a.k.a throwing in some mega surprises for you and your crew. To celebrate, we have a list of wine experiences to pick from, grape stomping to get knee-deep into, wine master classes and more! But we aren’t just stopping there. We are a music festival after all, and this year we’ve got a host of national and international bands and artists bringing their magic to the main stage. While you’re here, we’ll let you in on a little secret. Headlining SulaFest 2020 is British-band Hot Chip, who will be playing in India for the first time! And if you’re still looking for more adventure, you can camp at our campsite and allow nature’s music to put you to sleep. Finally, for every ticket sold, Sula Vineyards will plant a tree on your behalf, making it India’s first sustainable music festival. What’re you waiting for? Book your tickets today
