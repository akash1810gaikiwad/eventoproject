Artfully designed stages, mind-blowing art shows, world-class food, and the very best DJ’s in the world place Belgium’s Tomorrowland in a class of its own. This is hands down one of the biggest and best EDM festivals on the planet, attracting over 1,000 DJs and thousands of music lovers from all corners of the globe to the small town of Boom.
Each July, the scenic De Schorre Park between Brussels and Antwerp morphs into a magical wonderland, complete with lovely natural scenery and sensational scenography . From dawn till dusk, its 16 stages are pumping with all kinds of electronic music, and Dreamville – the festival’s themed camping ground – is filled with cute little shops and delicious food stand
